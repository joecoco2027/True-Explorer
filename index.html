About Mr. Abdulaziz Mafta

Abdulaziz Mafita is a passionate Economics and Tax professional, storyteller, coach, traveler, and truth-seeker. By day, he serves as a Tax Management Officer at the Tanzania Revenue Authority (TRA); by calling, he walks alongside people on their journey of growth, healing, and self-discovery.With a growing footprint in the world of personal development, coaching, and thought leadership, Abdulaziz blends professional discipline with deep insight, helping others unlock purpose, navigate transitions, and connect with meaning. With a strong foundation in Economics, Sociology, and Tax Administration, he brings clarity, insight, and action-oriented coaching to individuals and groups seeking purpose, career direction, or emotional resilience. His mission is to help others rise from confusion to clarity, from burnout to boldness, from potential to purpose. But he’s also an explorer of landscapes, culture, and the human spirit. Through content that blends raw reactions, deep wisdom, visual journeys across Tanzania, and liberation stories from African history, he connects with audiences who are hungry for both truth and transformation. Whether he’s speaking to a room, coaching one-on-one, or sharing stories from the Serengeti, one thing remains constant: he’s here to inspire reflection, empower growth, and spark meaningful change.
